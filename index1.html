Space

Space is dark, it is really dark in Space. Space is full of different things such as stars, planets, quasers, black holes, galaxies, and solar systems. Space is all in a place called the UNIVERSE. It was made 13.8 billion years ago, and our Solar System was made from a cloud of interstellar gas and dust that collapsed, resulting in a Solar nebula. Our Solar System is in the cluster Orion Spur in the Milky Way. The Milky Way is a spiral galaxy, and after a few billion years, we are going to crash into the Andromeda Galaxy. Our black hole holds together our Milky Way, it is called Sagittarius*A Star.
